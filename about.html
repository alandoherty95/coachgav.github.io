Get to Know Gavin

Read about Gavin's favourite ways to exercise and his passion for helping people to find their fitness.

Finding my fitness

I got into fitness when I was 12 years old. My friend owned weights and ever since it all I've ever wanted to pursue. Over the years, I've accumulated a wealth of knowledge in all aspects of fitness which allows me to be the perfect mentor to others.

My favourite ways to exercise

I love to split my gym routines as I feel this is most effective and enjoyable for anyone seeking to do 3 or more gym days. This will include every major muscle group and changes every 6 weeks for me as it will for my clients. Each day consists of some sort of cardiovascular exercise at some stage throughout the day which allows me to be more flexible with my food.

My love for personal training

I love what I do because not only does it help one to better themselves but it also gives me a great sense of satisfaction and achievement when I see the difference it can make to someone's life. This is my way of life and this is the way of life I want my clients to have. Happy healthy and enjoying what you do.

My passion for helping people become fitter and healthier

I love the well-being, confidence and better quality of life that my program can give to people. I love the thank you messages I get when people are in the best shape they have been in years under my guidance. I love everything about my work and the results people get from it.

Other ways to stay fit and healthy

I love to surf, walk or go running. I'm a sport fanatic - you name a sport I play it. I have played Gaelic Football for the Limerick Minors. I completed on the popular RTE show Ireland's Fittest Family. I play alot of Gaelic Football, Hurling Soccer and Rugby. When I'm not playing it, I love to watch it. This was also a big help to me in pursueing my fitness career.
